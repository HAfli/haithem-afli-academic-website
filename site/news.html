  • 2026-07-17 · publication
    New IWSLT 2026 paper on low-resource cascaded speech translation
    A paper by Pournima Sonawane and Haithem Afli was published at IWSLT 2026. The work examines robust cascaded speech translation for Bhojpuri–Hindi and Irish–English, combining Whisper-based ASR with NLLB-200 translation and evaluating routing strategies for low-resource pairs. Authoritative source
  • 2026-06 · leadership
    Dr Haithem Afli joins Rinn Artificial Intelligence as Institutional Co-Lead at MTU and Deputy Theme Lead
    Dr Haithem Afli has taken on new research leadership roles within Rinn Artificial Intelligence – Research & Innovation in Data Science and AI. He serves as Institutional Co-Lead at MTU, Deputy Theme Lead for Inclusive Language Model & Translation Methods, and Principal Investigator. His work in the centre focuses on inclusive and multilingual language models, translation methods, culturally aware AI, reliable evaluation and human-centred approaches to artificial intelligence. Authoritative source
