    New IWSLT 2026 paper on low-resource cascaded speech translation
    A paper by Pournima Sonawane and Haithem Afli was published at IWSLT 2026. The work examines robust cascaded speech translation for Bhojpuri–Hindi and Irish–English, combining Whisper-based ASR with NLLB-200 translation and evaluating routing strategies for low-resource pairs.
