  • 2026-07-17 — New IWSLT 2026 paper on low-resource cascaded speech translation
    A paper by Pournima Sonawane and Haithem Afli was published at IWSLT 2026. The work examines robust cascaded speech translation for Bhojpuri–Hindi and Irish–English, combining Whisper-based ASR with NLLB-200 translation and evaluating routing strategies for low-resource pairs. Source
  • 2026-01 — PTEB accepted at EACL 2026
    Manuel Frank and Haithem Afli published PTEB, a dynamic text-embedding evaluation protocol using stochastic paraphrasing at evaluation time, at EACL 2026 (Long Papers). Source
