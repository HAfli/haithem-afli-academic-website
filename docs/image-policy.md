# Image & Copyright Policy
Use only: images owned by or licensed to Dr Afli; official institutional images with permission; photographs explicitly approved for public use; publication figures whose licence permits reuse; project logos under their guidelines.
Never: scrape/republish images from LinkedIn, Google Scholar, social media, publisher or third-party sites; use AI-generated imagery of Dr Afli; assume an online image is reusable because it is public.
On receipt of an approved photograph: strip unnecessary EXIF; generate optimised web versions (e.g. 280px and 560px WebP/JPEG); write meaningful alt text; keep the original privately; document source and permission here. Until then the site uses a neutral placeholder (no photograph required by the design).
Every published image must have meaningful alternative text.
